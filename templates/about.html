About Cigarbox


A smokin' fast personal photostream

Cigarbox is a light weight, open source, personal photostream and archive. It was designed to mimic the photo graph of Flickr (photos, tags, sets, galleries) and provide a simple and friendly interface for friends and family to browse your photos. Photos are generally displayed in order of date-added, with the exception of photosets, which are in order of date-taken.

For more information or to download and use on your own site, visit the project site at github.com/n8foo/cigarbox
